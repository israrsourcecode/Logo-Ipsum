Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your data when you engage with our journal publishing services. By using our services, you acknowledge and accept these policies. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We collect personal information that you provide during registration, manuscript submission, or communication with us. This includes your full name, email address, affiliation, and contact details, all of which are required.
  • Manuscript Information: When you submit a manuscript, we collect data related to your research, including the manuscript content, illustrations, tables, and supporting materials.
  • Peer Review Information: We collect author details, email addresses, peer reviews, and any additional information provided during the review process.
  • Usage Data: We collect data on how you interact with our website, including your IP address, browser type, and visit timestamps. This helps us improve our services and enhance user experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • Manuscript Processing: We use your personal and manuscript information to manage the manuscript review and publication process.
  • Peer Review Management: We use collected data to facilitate the peer review process, including reviewer invitations, assignments, and feedback to authors.
  • Communication: We use your personal data to inform you about journal updates, newsletters, and relevant information. You may opt out of these communications at any time.
  • Website Improvement: We analyze website usage data to improve our services and user experience.
  • Compliance and Legal Obligations: We may process your information to comply with legal obligations, enforce our terms, and protect against fraud or misuse.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your personal information with third-party service providers who help us operate our platform. These providers are legally bound to use your data only for authorized purposes.
  • Peer Reviewers: Manuscript details are shared with peer reviewers under strict confidentiality, solely for review purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to prevent fraudulent or unlawful activity.
  • Business Transfers: If our company undergoes a merger, acquisition, or sale, your data may be transferred to the new entity. You will be notified and have the right to decline such transfers.
